### Summary
The Outcomes Conference, hosted by the Christian Leadership Alliance (CLA), is the largest annual gathering of Christian nonprofit professionals in the country. The 2026 theme is **"Multiply"** — equipping leaders to multiply their kingdom impact. The conference features 100+ learning experiences across nine core educational tracks and influences more than 180,000 leaders each year. PRAY.COM participates as a **Premier-level sponsor** with a Grand Foyer booth and two speaking sessions.

### Audience
The conference draws Christian nonprofit professionals and leaders at all organizational levels — executives, board members, development directors, and ministry staff — who seek to enhance their leadership, stewardship, and mission impact.

- **72%** — Christian/Parachurch Ministries
- **15%** — Educational Institutions
