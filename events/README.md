## Document Structure

Each event document follows the standard PRAY.COM event template with these sections:

1. **SNAPSHOT** - Quick overview with key details
2. **MISSION & SUCCESS** - Goals, metrics, and success criteria
3. **THEME & STORY** - Event messaging and visual identity
4. **AUDIENCE, PROMOTIONAL & DISTRIBUTION LAYER** - Target audience and promotion
5. **EXPERIENCE & CONVERSION** - Attendee journey and conversion mechanics
6. **OPERATIONAL READINESS** - Staffing, run of show, systems, assets
7. **OWNERSHIP, RISKS & WATCHLIST** - Decision authority and risk management
8. **POST-EVENT PLAN** - Follow-up commitments and retrospective
9. **TO-DO LIST WITH DATES** - Detailed task timeline for event preparation

## Key Preparation Milestones

For each event, the to-do list includes these standard phases:
- **12-16 weeks before**: Initial planning, goal setting, team assignment
- **8-10 weeks before**: Content development, outreach planning
- **4-6 weeks before**: Final preparation, promotional push
- **1-2 weeks before**: Final confirmations, team briefings
- **Event week**: Execution
- **Post-event**: Follow-up, CRM updates, retrospective
- **30 days post**: Final reporting and ROI analysis
